Introduction

In this era of modern world, where everything is digitalized why to keep the hustle of carrying traditional business card. Digital business card, also known as virtual business cards or electronic business cards is a one stop solution which contains important business and contact information that can be accessed by anyone from anywhere and can be shared online as well.

Goal

The goal is to make cost-effective, interactive, and environmentally friendly and easy to create Business cards that are fully customizable, allowing user to create a vibrant, unique card where one can choose layout and color scheme of their choice that matches your personal or company needs and update it on a click. Also, these cards will never get lost!

UI/UX
The aim is to create user friendly web application for the creation of digital business cards with professional design and layout that will help to make a better portfolio.
Features
One can make one or more digital cards for business or personal use. The dashboard will allow users to analyze how many views or interactions have each of your card received.
